Welcome to Vilufushi Island!

Vilufushi is a beautiful island located at Tha Atoll, south central province, Maldives. The island was completely destroyed during the 2004 tsunami disaster, but was later reconstructed. Now, the island is considered as a safe island and it's even more beautiful! Come visit to discover more! Sign up to win a ticket!
