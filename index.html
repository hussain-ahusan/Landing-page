Welcome to Vilufushi Island!

Vilufushi is a beautiful island located at Tha Atoll, south central province, Maldives. The island was completely destroyed during the 2004 tsunami disaster, but was later reconstructed and repaired to suite living conditions. Now, the island is considered as a safe island and it's even more beautiful than ever! Come visit to discover more! Sign up to book your trip!
